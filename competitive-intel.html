Competitive Intelligence — Panama

Updated 2026-06-23 · Next review Q3 2026 (Sep) · Owner: Abraham · INTERNAL — do not share

The one finding that matters: no major Panama player is a custom-home builder for foreign buyers. They are resale brokerages, global franchises, or listing portals. The only direct builder competitor is Pedasi Developments (a few pre-designed models, limited customization, sold through a third party, almost no brand). Sora's lane — fully bespoke, foreign-buyer custom homes, founder-led, transparent, English-first, with a configurator — is essentially uncontested. The fight is for attention, trust, and the same buyer's inbox, not for the product.

Data confidence: Panama has no public MLS, so "homes sold" figures are self-reported marketing — treated as claims, not facts. Numbers are point-in-time (June 2026) with sources per card. Screenshots of each competitor's homepage / listing / funnel are pending (browser tooling was offline at capture) and slot in next pass.

What Sora should do

  1. Own the category nobody else does. Lead with custom home, built for foreign buyers, fixed price, you decide from home. No competitor occupies this — do not dilute into generic "real estate."
  2. Out-niche on SEO; don't fight the portals. Encuentra24 (34K listings) and Compreoalquile own the transactional head terms. Win the English foreign-buyer + custom-build long tail: "build a custom home in Panama," "buying property in Panama as a foreigner," "[Boquete/Pedasí/Volcán] custom home builder." Mirror Compreoalquile's clean keyword URLs and Encuentra24's bilingual /en//es/ paths.
  3. Close the proof gap. Every competitor is vague on track record. Publish verifiable completed projects, real timelines, before/after, signed fixed prices — credibility no one else offers (without overclaiming).
  4. Win the video/social vacuum. Best in market is ~2–9K followers; nobody runs a build-journey channel and nobody is on TikTok. A "watch your house get built" engine is wide open.
  5. Beat them on funnel. Everyone is WhatsApp-form basic. Sora's 3D configurator + booked consult + weekly video walk-throughs is a structural edge.
  6. Weaponize anti-"horror story" trust. Pedasí/Azuero copy references buyers burned by bad builders. Fixed price, milestone/escrow payments, all-risk insurance, warranty, bilingual PM is the antidote — make it loud.
  7. Steal what works: Panama Equity's education funnel (quarterly market reports, 20K email list, <24h follow-up SLA, relocation webinar); Casa Solution's email-alert capture; Inside Panama's multilingual angle (German/Afrikaans); RE/MAX's "Why buy in Panama" tax/visa pillar.

Profiles

Casa Solution 4

Resale brokerage · the dominant brand in Boquete/Volcán/Bocas (Sora's highland turf)
  • Coverage: Boquete + David + Chiriquí beaches; expanded to Volcán, Bocas, Azuero, Panama City. Full overlap with Sora's highlands.
  • Listings: markets "2,500+" (aggregated); ~300 signed Boquete; claims "5x any competitor."
  • Claims: "1,300+ transactions," "$100M Chiriquí sales / 4 yrs," "#1 Boquete." Self-reported.
  • Team: 6 named + several more → ~10–20.
  • Strength: aged authoritative domain, strong local SEO, email alerts, multi-office, team-trust framing.
  • Social: IG 2,288 · FB 2,403 · LinkedIn 87 · YouTube (small) · no TikTok.
  • Gap: resale only, listing-spam content, EN/ES-only social, no TikTok, vague "2,500+." Sora wins on build + education + founder brand.
casasolution.com · /our-stats-explained · /boquete-houses · IG @casa.solution.boquete

Pedasi Developments 4

The only true custom-home builder competitor — Sora's closest analog
  • Coverage: Pedasí, Playa Venao, Cañas. Builder Gabriel Noguera.
  • Model: pre-designed models (Casa Sanoa/Indico/Casita) + lots; turnkey 6–10 mo; permits/architects A-to-Z; 1-yr warranty; staged payments.
  • Track record: no quantified homes-built claim — a real credibility gap.
  • Weakness: explicitly "limited customization"; relies on Ken Norton / Panama Properties to sell; ~zero social; brochure site.
  • Gap: Sora owns the fully bespoke lane they cap; lead with proof; fill social vacuum; go direct-to-buyer.
pedasidevelopments.com · panama-properties.com/post/want-to-build-in-panama

Panama Equity 3

Resale brokerage · SEO/content authority, "#1 in Pedasí"
  • Coverage: countrywide; office in Pedasí; associates in Bocas/Coronado/Boquete; PC-centric. Founder Kent Davis (WSJ/Time/FT).
  • Funnel: <24h follow-up SLA, 20,000+ email list, relocation webinar, quarterly market reports, WhatsApp Business.
  • SEO: strong — big blog + market reports + city guides; self-claims #1–2 Google.
  • Social: YT 11K · IG 9,096 · FB 4,630 · LinkedIn 1,437.
  • Gap: brokerage not builder; thin custom-build content; no proof totals. Copy their education funnel + YouTube tours.
panamaequity.com · /resources/market-insights · YouTube/IG

Panama Sovereign Realty 3

Resale brokerage · strongest Pedasí brand + referral gatekeeper

RE/MAX Panama 3

Encuentra24 3

Portal · owns the generic property SERPs (the SEO benchmark to study, not beat)
