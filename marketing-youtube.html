How to structure the deals (warmest/cheapest first)

  1. Affiliate / referral (start here, near-zero risk). Unique link + promo code per creator. Pay on outcome under Track B of legal/acuerdo-de-referido.md: a flat fee per closing ($3–5K) or a finder's fee. Perfect for the archetype creators, they mention Sora naturally; you only pay when it works.
  2. Paid sponsor read (for ones with audience who already do sponsorships). A 60–90s host-read; ballpark $500–$2,500 for small/mid Panama creators. Use after a referral proves a creator's audience converts. Always a unique URL.
  3. Pay-to-feature (I Go Panama, the tours) — they sell placements; just buy one.
  4. Host-a-creator-at-a-build (highest ROI, later). Invite a creator to film at a Sora site. Evergreen "we visited Sora" video + reusable b-roll. Dedicated videos convert 3–5x better than integrations.

Sequence: lock 3–4 archetype creators on referral (free until they produce), add a paid feature (I Go Panama / a tour) to test paid reach, then graduate the best performer to a hosted dedicated video.

The pitch (ready to send)

Short, personal, honest. Open with a real detail from their channel. Never overclaim a track record. No exclusivity, no scripts.

Our own channel (what to film)

YouTube is the #2 search engine, "retire/build/cost in Panama" has durable demand, and the founder living in Chiriquí is the unfair advantage. Founder-led, honest, same voice as the blog. Realistic: 1 video/week + Shorts cut from it. First videos, mapped to keywords we already target:

  1. "What it really costs to build a house in Panama (2026)" — the moat keyword.
  2. "The land mistake that costs foreigners their savings" — titled vs derecho posesorio.
  3. "Boquete vs Pedasí: mountains or coast?" — drone both.
  4. "Volcán: the cheaper highlands."
  5. "How a $200K home gets you Panama residency."
  6. Build vlog series (once ground breaks) — the strongest trust asset.
  7. Founder POV / day-in-the-life — feeds the ad retargeting pool.

Phone + gimbal + a drone; 8–12 min search videos; cut each into 2–3 Shorts. Descriptions link the matching blog post + the lead form; end-screen CTA to the cost guide / a call.
