El cuello de botellaThe bottleneck

El interés es enorme. El camino es confuso.The interest is huge. The path is confusing.

Por eso la mayoría se queda mirando, no comprando.Anyone buying from abroad runs into foreigner markups, unverified listings, paperwork in another language and no builder with a fixed price. So most people keep watching instead of buying.
