Backlink playbook

Backlinks are the biggest off-page lever for a new domain. Relevance and trust beat volume. This is all white-hat, all things a real Panama home builder legitimately earns, and it's executable this week.

Sora Casas·Internal·June 2026·Source: sora-real-estate/16-backlinks.md

The principle

Earn links the way a credible business does: get listed where buyers look, get quoted by journalists, get on podcasts, publish data worth citing, and partner with the people already serving your buyer. One link from International Living or a relocation-tour blog beats 500 directory links. Never buy links or use private blog networks, one cleanup later costs more than the links were ever worth.

The order that matters (relevance × authority)

  1. Partner links (people we already work with) — highest relevance, easiest.
  2. Original-data PR (the Sora Build Index) — scalable, high-authority, repeatable.
  3. Podcasts + guest posts — relevance plus a real audience.
  4. HARO / journalist sourcing — high-authority news links, ongoing.
  5. Foundational citations — directories + profiles. Low value each, but quick trust signals.

Do this week

Day 1–2 · Foundations (you control these)

  • Google Business Profile for Sora Casas (Pedasí or Boquete). Free; a real citation + Maps presence + a link. Single highest-value quick win.
  • Citations / profiles with identical name/address/phone + the soracasas.com link: ExpatExchange, Expat.com (Panama), InterNations, Crunchbase, LinkedIn company page, clean X/Instagram/Facebook business profiles. ~6 links, ~1 hour.

Day 2–3 · Partner links (warm, high relevance)

Every business we work with is a natural link exchange. Ask each for a partners/resources link; offer the same back from a Sora "trusted partners" page.

  • The relocation-tour operators (Panama Relocation Tours, Retire in Panama Tours).
  • The attorneys, brokers (Casa Solution, Inside Panama RE), and boutique hotels from the Chiriquí partner list.
  • ACOBIR member listing once membership is filed.

Day 3–5 · The data-PR play (strongest scalable angle)

The Sora Build Index (real per-region build costs) is original data, and data earns links because publications cite numbers they can't get elsewhere.

  • Package the cost data as a short, linkable "2026 Panama Build Cost Report" (the cost article is most of it).
  • Pitch it as a free resource to International Living, Live and Invest Overseas, Expatra, Escape Artist, The Wandering Investor.

Ongoing · HARO / journalist sourcing

  • Sign up for Featured.com, Qwoted, and Help a B2B Writer (the current HARO replacements). Answer 1–2 queries/day where Abraham is a credible source (retiring abroad, Panama, building/real estate). Each accepted answer = a link from a real media site. Compounds; highest-authority repeatable source.

Week 1 · Send 3 podcast + 3 guest-post pitches

  • Podcasts: DoPanama Podcast, The Expat Money Show, Live and Invest Overseas, Going Tropical, FIRE/expat shows. Angle: "the builder who tells foreigners the honest version, titled-land traps and all."
  • Guest posts: one genuinely useful article (not promotional) to expat blogs. Best angle: "The land-title mistake that costs foreign buyers their savings."

Outreach templates

Podcast pitch: Hi [name], I build custom homes for foreigners in Panama and spend most of my time talking people out of the mistakes that cost them, mainly buying land that turns out not to be titled. Your audience would get real value from the unvarnished version: what it costs, the visa-by-property routes, and the traps. Happy to come on and give specifics, no pitch.
Data-PR pitch: Hi [name], we track real per-region construction costs in Panama (Pedasí, Boquete, Volcán, Bocas) and publish them quarterly as the Sora Build Index. You're welcome to cite the numbers, here's the report: [link]. Happy to give a quote or break it down by region.
Partner link ask: Hi [name], we send buyers your way and would love to list you on our trusted-partners page, mind adding Sora to your resources list too? Here's our link: [link].

Don't

No paid link packages, PBNs, mass-submitted directories, comment/forum link spam, or exact-match-anchor over-optimization. These trip spam systems and are a liability. Don't chase domain-rating numbers; chase links a real buyer might actually click.

Measure

Track referring domains monthly in Search Console (Links report) + a free Ahrefs Webmaster Tools account. Target for a new site: 5–10 quality referring domains in the first 90 days, weighted toward partners + media, not directories.
